The Story

Madame Butterfly tells the poignant tale of love and betrayal, set in the beautiful landscapes of Nagasaki, Japan. The story revolves around Cio-Cio-San, a young geisha who marries an American naval officer, only to face heartbreak and tragedy.

About Giacomo Puccini

Puccini, one of the greatest opera composers, brought his genius to Madame Butterfly with a rich score that captures the essence of its characters and setting.

The Legacy

Madame Butterfly has captivated audiences worldwide with its emotional depth and exquisite music. It remains a cornerstone of the operatic repertoire.
